About Me

Video editor and storyteller with a passion for visual narratives

Alex Morgan

I'm a video editor and motion designer with a passion for visual storytelling. With over 8 years of experience in the industry, I've had the privilege of working with brands like Nike, Tesla, and HBO.

My approach combines technical precision with creative intuition, allowing me to craft compelling narratives that resonate with audiences. Whether it's a commercial, documentary, or narrative film, I bring the same level of dedication and attention to detail.

My Philosophy

I believe that editing is the invisible art that brings a story to life. It's about finding the perfect rhythm, the right emotional beats, and the visual flow that keeps viewers engaged. Every cut, every transition, and every effect should serve the story and enhance the viewer's experience.
